Bochs x86 PC Emulation Developer's Email List

To provide a forum for the development of the Bochs x86 PC emulation software, and to keep interested parties apprised of developments, there is a developer's email list available to anyone willing to join. After joining, you may email to bochs-developers@redhat.com. Your email will be sent to EVERYONE on the list. Keep in mind, if you have questions regarding licensing or specific business oriented topics, email me directly at kevin@bochs.com. In this case, your email will only be received by me (Kevin Lawton).
