Bochs x86 PC Emulation Developer's Email List

To provide a forum for the development of the Bochs x86 PC emulation software, and to keep interested parties apprised of developments, there is a developer's email list available to anyone willing to join. After joining, you may email to bochs-developers@lists.sourceforge.net. Your email will be sent to EVERYONE on the list. 
How to subscribe to the bochs-developers mailing list:

Subscribe via the web:
1. Go to the bochs-developers info page.
2. Type your email address and a password into the blanks and click Subscribe.
3. In a few minutes you will receive a confirmation email.  Follow 
   the directions to complete the subscription process.

Subscribe via email:
1. Send an email to bochs-developers-request@lists.sourceforge.net
   with the subject line "subscribe".  Don't forget the "-request" part
   or your subscribe message will go to 300 people.
2. In a few minutes you will receive a confirmation email.  Follow 
   the directions to complete the subscription process.

When you have joined the list, send email to:
  bochs-developers@lists.sourceforge.net
